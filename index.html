Why Is My Face Puffy?

🧂 High Sodium Intake

Excess sodium can cause water retention, leading to a swollen, puffy appearance and bloating.

😴 Poor Sleep

Lack of quality sleep can worsen fluid retention and contribute to under-eye bags and fatigue.

😩 Chronic Stress

Long-term stress can affect sleep, appetite, exercise habits, and overall health.

🚶 Lack of Activity

Limited movement may contribute to weight gain and reduced cardiovascular fitness.

💧 Dehydration

Not drinking enough water can make water retention and bloating more noticeable.

🌱 Gut Health Neglect

Poor dietary habits may contribute to bloating, excess gas, digestive discomfort, and low energy.

Common Signs You Need a Reset

  • Frequent bloating
  • Puffy face in the morning
  • Low energy levels
  • Poor sleep quality
  • Excess gas
  • Weight gain
  • Feeling sluggish throughout the day
